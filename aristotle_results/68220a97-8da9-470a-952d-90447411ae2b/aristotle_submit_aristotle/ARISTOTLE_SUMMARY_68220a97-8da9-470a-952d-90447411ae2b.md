# Summary of changes
## Per-Denominator Decomposition of R(p): Exploration Results

Added to `RequestProject/SignTheorem.lean`:

### New Definitions
- **`crossTermDenom p b`**: Per-denominator cross term Σ_{a coprime b} D(a/b)·δ(a/b)
- **`shiftSquaredDenom p b`**: Per-denominator shift-squared sum Σ_{a coprime b} δ(a/b)²  
- **`corrRatioDenom p b`**: Per-denominator ratio R_b = crossTermDenom / shiftSquaredDenom

### Computational Verification for p = 13
- **`corrRatioDenom_ge_neg_half_13`**: R_b ≥ -1/2 for ALL denominators b (verified by `native_decide`)
- **`corrRatioDenom_13_5`**: R_5 = -1/2 exactly (boundary case)
- **`corrRatioDenom_13_7_strict`**: R_7 > -1/2 (strict, ensuring overall R > -1/2)
- **`corrRatio_13_gt_neg_half`**: Overall R(13) = 813/15872 > -1/2

### Key Finding: Per-Denominator Approach Fails for General p
- **`corrRatioDenom_19_16_violation`**: At p=19, R₁₆ = -5/6 < -1/2 ❌
- **`corrRatioDenom_19_17_violation`**: At p=19, R₁₇ = -7/12 < -1/2 ❌
- **`corrRatio_19_gt_neg_half`**: Despite violations, overall R(19) > -1/2 still holds

### Summary of Findings

| Prime p | R_b ≥ -1/2 for all b? | Violating denominators |
|---------|----------------------|----------------------|
| 13 | ✅ (equality at b=5) | None |
| 19 | ❌ | b=16 (-5/6), b=17 (-7/12) |
| 31 | ✅ | None |
| 43 | ❌ | b=37, b=40, b=41 |
| 47 | ❌ | b=3, b=9, b=15, b=22, b=41, b=43, b=45 |

**Conclusion**: The per-denominator bound R_b > -1/2 does NOT hold in general. Large denominators b (close to p) can have very negative R_b values (as low as -3 at p=47, b=45). However, these denominators have small shift-squared weights, so the overall weighted average R still exceeds -1/2. A general proof would need to bound the weighted sum Σ w_b · R_b rather than each R_b individually. All new code compiles successfully via `native_decide`.
