## WINNER: ANGLE 3 — DICHOTOMY THEOREM (write up NOW)

**Theorem (Dichotomy, Unconditional).**
Let ρ*_T be the zero with maximal real part β*_T among zeros with 0 < Im(ρ) < T.
Then: F_N(γ*_T) ≥ c · N^{2β*_T - 1} · (γ*_T)² / (log N)²

- If RH holds → β* = 1/2 → F concentrates at all zeros (full conditional applies)
- If RH fails → β* > 1/2 → F concentrates at most off-line zero with N^{2β*-1} → ∞

"The spectroscope is a zero-detector REGARDLESS of RH."
