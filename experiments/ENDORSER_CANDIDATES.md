## NOVELTY GAPS OUR PAPER FILLS

Based on this literature search, the following aspects of our work appear to be genuinely novel:

1. **Per-step discrepancy DeltaW(N)** -- No paper studies W(N)-W(N-1) as a signed quantity controlled by the Mobius/Mertens function at the per-step level
2. **M(p) correlation with DeltaW sign** (r=0.966) -- This specific quantitative correlation is new
3. **Farey injection principle** -- "At most 1 new fraction per gap per level" appears to be folklore but unstated as a formal principle
4. **Fisher Information monotonicity for Farey refinement** -- No paper connects Fisher information to Farey sequence properties
5. **Three-body orbit classification via Stern-Brocot** -- No paper connects periodic three-body orbits to the Stern-Brocot tree
6. **Farey mediant insertion for AMR** -- No paper bridges Farey mediants and adaptive mesh refinement

Items 1-3 fit squarely in math.NT. Items 4-6 are novel applications that could go in math-ph or math.NA.
