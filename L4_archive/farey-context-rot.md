# Farey Context Rot

Material here is retained for warning/provenance, not active reasoning.

## Avoid

- Pre-`mu_f(p^2)` C1 numerical claims unless independently reverified.
- Old pure-rank-only W2 language; use W2 prime.
- Delta pointwise half-value framing.
- General cross-form universality claims for C1 second moments.
- Raw Sym2/Petersson proportionality as an active claim.
- Turan A2 as theorem; it is open after citation failure.
- Chebyshev sign theorem; it has a counterexample.
- Old active-compute assumptions about M5B or EXO.
- Failed M2 six-byte stubs.
- Unrelated 3DGS, AMR, biotech, and other non-Farey files from old experiment sprawl.
- Old routine state, cron logs, scheduled-agent definitions, and live queue files.

## Provenance Rule

If a future task needs old evidence, fetch from `raw/farey-archive/` using the manifest hash. Do not promote old material until it is reconciled with [[L2_facts/farey-claim-ledger]].
